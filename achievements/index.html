Achievements made by Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju Adewale



  • Became CEO of Meisophant Digital Services at the age of 11 years — establishing him as the world’s youngest CEO.
  • Founded Meisophant Digital Services (initially an online company) on Sunday, 28 July 2024
  • Began conceiving the idea of Meisophant at the age of 9, originally as a gaming-company concept.
  • Shifted the company’s focus by age 10 to become a developer-tool company rather than only a gaming company.
  • Through Meisophant Developers he is prepared to launch tools/platforms such as TekSpot and others.
  • Relaunched the official Meisophant website on 2 November 2025, and got it listed on Google the same day — marking another record.
  • Maintains residence and operations in Lagos, Nigeria, demonstrating entrepreneurial activity from a young age in the tech sector.
  • Recognised in a listing of the “Top 15 world’s youngest CEOs”.
